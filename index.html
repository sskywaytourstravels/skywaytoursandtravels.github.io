Land Transport

The pioneer of Express Distribution in India, Skyway Tours & Travels continues to innovate new offerings to address demands for express logistics in India. We ensure time-definite deliveries through our robust network supported by 'Hub and Spoke' model, which is intended to minimize the distance travelled and strategic management of logistics. Our fleet of over 100 containerised vehicles covers the entire country proving a comprehensive range of fast, efficient & express transport and logistics services. As long as there's a road to it, there isn't a place in this country we can't deliver to or pick-up from! This offering enables 'Just in Time' and lean supply chain requirements of the customers. This service also allows customers to plan their in transit inventory – an important input for advanced materials and distribution requirements planning. We fully utilize our infrastructure, technology resources, research & development, network, fleet and our ecosystem of partners to provide an unrivalled express logistics experience in India.
